Opening hours

We serve lunch and dinner six days a week. The bar opens 30 minutes before each service for oysters, cocktails and aperitifs.

During Czech public holidays we usually open in the evening only — check our Instagram for the current week.

Dress code

Welcome

Smart casual

A well-cut shirt, a soft jacket, a dress, a good knit. The room is candlelit — everything looks better here.

Not welcome

No sportswear or caps

Tracksuits, baseball caps and beachwear are kindly refused at the door. Smart trainers are absolutely fine.

05

Accessibility

The dining room is on street level with a single 6 cm threshold at the entrance. We have one accessible washroom. Service dogs are welcome.

If you have a hearing aid, mobility, dietary or sensory consideration we should know about, please email info@oyster.cz at the time of booking — we will prepare the room accordingly.

Parking is available at Kotva and Náměstí Republiky garages, both within 200 metres.
