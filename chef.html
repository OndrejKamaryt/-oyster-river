Section 06 · Chef

The kitchen

Executive Chef Vojtěch Petržela leads River & Oyster with precision, discipline and a clear ambition: to move modern Czech gastronomy forward from a room built around fish, fire, stocks and sauces.

About the chef Philosophy Concept Ingredients Sustainability Achievements Team & suppliers
01

About the chef

Vojtěch is one of the most talented chefs of his generation and one of the most ambitious figures in Czech gastronomy. His goal is not only to cook excellent food, but to build a restaurant that can stand among the gastronomic destinations of Europe.

Every day is guided by one idea: to be better than yesterday. He studies techniques, looks to the best kitchens in the world and believes that excellence is built from thousands of small details guests may not name, but always feel.

02

Philosophy

Technical precision, deep respect for ingredients and uncompromising craft. Vojtěch does not use complexity to impress; he believes the highest form of luxury is mastering the simple things perfectly.

03

The concept

River

Oysters & river

Oysters, river fish, roe, stocks and sauces. Each dish is built with a clear reason.

Fire

Fire

Open flame, smoke, grilling and careful reductions give the kitchen its depth.

Season

Season & responsibility

Czech herbs, fruit, vegetables and dairy meet careful sourcing and responsible use of each product.

04

Local ingredients

Where possible, the kitchen works from the Czech landscape: seasonal vegetables, herbs, fruit, dairy and honey. Beyond Bohemia, we choose named producers for oysters and fish whose quality can carry the plate.

Seasonal vegetables Forest herbs Czech fruit Dairy Honey Named oyster & fish producers
05

Sustainability

Freshness decides the menu, not convenience. The kitchen uses the whole product where possible: skins, bones, bellies and trim become stocks, sauces, crisps, smoke or staff meals.

06

Selected achievements

  • Czech Chef of the Year 2019 / 2020 — winner
  • Czech National Culinary Team member
  • Global Chef Challenge World Final, Abu Dhabi 4th place
  • Gastro Junior & KU Young Chef multiple winner
  • International competitions, Erfurt & Luxembourg gold medals
07

Team & suppliers

Behind Vojtěch is a small, focused team and a short list of trusted partners: fishermen, oyster growers, farmers, dairies, winemakers and importers who understand that precision begins before the product reaches the kitchen.
